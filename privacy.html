Privacy Policy for IIOU

Last updated: June 14, 2026

At IIOU, accessible from https://iiou.vercel.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors.

This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by IIOU and how we use it.

The information is used strictly to manage the IOU (I Owe You) ledger and to facilitate secure payments via the Pi Network SDK.
